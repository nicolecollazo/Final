Nicole Collazo Santana

I am a 20 year old Journalism + Design major at The New School, originally from Caguas, Puerto Rico. I love writing, drinking coffee, and reading art books (or anything else art related.)

Spanish is my first language but I am completely fluent in English and I took Italian 101, as I lived in Rome for 5 months.

Currently, I am a Contributing Latina Writer for PopSugar. Some of my works have been also published on the New School Free Press and HoneySuckle Magazine.
