About me

Puerto Rico

New York

Rome

Growing up in Puerto Rico, my mom would always take me to museums and started me out in different art classes like painting, music, and dance. I stuck primarily to ballet but I always found in my free time a passion for reading, which would then inspire me to write. Even when I was a kid and didn’t know how to hold a pen, I would narrate stories to my mom and make her write them. In high school, I was oriented more to math and sciences yet I found a way to implement these artistic passions of mine in everything I did.

Attending a liberal arts college in New York City, Eugene Lang College at The New School, I am currently in my third year in the Journalism + Design department. Here I have developed skills such as:

  1. Research
  2. Interviewing
  3. Developing digital and print content
  4. Organization within short and strict deadlines
  5. Basic Web Design

Writing is everything to me, even if it's just to-do lists or journaling. In my time as a student, I have written for the university’s newspaper as well as other publications. Take a look at my published works here.
